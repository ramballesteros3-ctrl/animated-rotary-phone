My Approach

How I think about product.

01
Understand the problem deeply
I spend disproportionate time in discovery — with users, stakeholders, and data — before writing a single requirement.
02
Define clear, measurable outcomes
Every initiative starts with a north-star metric and a hypothesis. I build to learn, then to scale.
03
Align early, ship iteratively
I bring engineering, design, compliance, and business together early — and deliver in iterations that earn trust.
04
Measure, learn, improve
Post-launch is where the real work begins. I track KPIs obsessively and treat every release as an input to the next.
